Test Article for Media Bias Analyzer

This is a test article to verify that the Media Bias Analyzer extension is working correctly with the new client-side prompt storage implementation.

The article discusses a fictional political event where both conservative and liberal viewpoints are presented. This allows us to test the bias analysis functionality.

Conservative politicians argue that lower taxes will stimulate economic growth and create jobs. They point to historical examples where tax cuts led to increased business investment.

Liberal politicians counter that higher taxes on the wealthy are necessary to fund social programs and reduce inequality. They cite studies showing that trickle-down economics has not benefited the middle class.

Both sides present their arguments with conviction, though some experts suggest a balanced approach might be more effective than either extreme position.

The debate continues with no clear resolution, as is often the case in complex policy discussions.
